Your Cycle
The app learns your rhythm as you tag period days.
Days 1–5 · Period
Menstrual
Prostaglandins cause cramps and fatigue. Gentle walks (15–20 min) reduce pain through circulation and endorphins. Rest is always valid — no guilt.
Energy level
Days 6–13 · Follicular
Follicular
Estrogen rises — energy surges, mood lifts, motivation returns. Best window to push duration and lock in new habits. You will feel it.
Energy level
Days 14–16 · Ovulation
Ovulation
Peak estrogen and testosterone. You feel unstoppable — full 50-minute walks with real effort. Best you will feel all cycle. Use it.
Energy level
Days 17–28 · Luteal
Luteal
Progesterone rises. Energy dips, PMS symptoms build toward end. Moderate pace on rough days. Consistent walking reduces PMS severity over time.

Tips
Small things that compound into big results.
💧
Hydrate extra on period & luteal days
Dehydration makes cramps and fatigue worse. One extra glass before your walk makes a real difference.
👟
Shoes matter more than speed
Shin splints kill habits fast. One good pair of walking shoes prevents this entirely.
🎵
Music adds ~10% to walk duration
A pump playlist makes 50 minutes feel like 35. Build it the night before.
🌡️
Fever or nausea = full rest, no guilt
Light movement during period is helpful. Pushing through illness is not. Tag it as sick.
⏰
Same time daily builds habit faster
Pick one anchor — morning or pre-dinner walk — and hold it for 3 weeks.
📈
Benefits show up around week 5–6
Lower resting heart rate, better sleep, stable energy. You will feel it before you measure it.
📅
Tag every period day you can
The more period days tagged, the more accurately the app knows your real cycle.
